# AGOS CIVILIZATION CANON

**VERSION**: 1.0  
**STATUS**: Official Canonical Vocabulary  
**DATE**: 2024

---

## MISSION

Establish one canonical language for the AGOS civilization.

Every Runtime, Agent, Capability, Provider, Workflow, Extension, Tool and Future Civilization shall use the same engineering vocabulary.

Canonical terminology replaces ambiguous terminology.

---

## SECTION 1: FOUNDATIONAL TERMS

### Mission
The highest-level engineering objective.

### Task
A unit of executable work.

### Plan
An ordered strategy for completing a Mission.

### Execution
The governed realization of a Plan.

### Capability
A reusable engineering ability.

### Provider
An external implementation of a Capability.

### Workflow
An executable engineering process.

### Policy
A governing rule.

### Artifact
A permanent engineering output.

### Evidence
Immutable proof.

### Knowledge
Validated engineering understanding.

### Decision
An evidence-backed engineering choice.

### Trust
Evidence-derived confidence.

### Benchmark
A measurable engineering reference.

### Doctrine
Engineering philosophy.

### Constitution
Supreme governing document.

### Reality
The final validation authority.

---

## SECTION 4: RULES

1. Every concept has one official name.
2. Every official name has one official definition.
3. Aliases are prohibited inside the Kernel.
4. Deprecated terms remain documented.
5. Canonical definitions are versioned.

---

## SECTION 5: NAMING STANDARD

Names shall be:
- **Explicit**: No abbreviations
- **Predictable**: Consistent patterns
- **Stable**: No frequent changes
- **Searchable**: Findable in code/docs
- **Technology-neutral**: Not tool-specific
- **Architecture-first**: Design-focused
- **Business-independent**: Domain-agnostic

---

## SECTION 6: LANGUAGE GOVERNANCE

No new engineering term may be introduced without:
1. Definition
2. Purpose
3. Relationship Graph
4. Compatibility Review
5. Approval
6. Publication

---

## SECTION 7: SEMANTIC STABILITY

Changing the meaning of an existing canonical term is prohibited.

If meaning changes:
1. Create a new canonical term
2. Deprecate the old one
3. Preserve historical compatibility

---

## SECTION 8: CANONICAL COMPLIANCE

Every document, API, Workflow, Knowledge Pack, Policy, Architecture Decision, Capability, Extension, SDK, and Agent shall use Canon terminology.

---

## OUTPUT

**AGOS Civilization Canon v1.0**

The entire civilization now shares one precise engineering language.

## FIVE FOUNDATIONAL PILLARS

After this document, the civilization has five immutable pillars:

1. **النواة (Kernel)**: The executive heart
2. **الدستور (Constitution)**: Supreme rules
3. **العقيدة الهندسية (Engineering Doctrine)**: Thinking methodology
4. **المرجع اللغوي (Civilization Canon)**: Official language
5. **التحقق من الواقع (Reality Validation)**: Final truth reference

These five form the permanent identity of the civilization. Everything else may evolve over time without losing consistency or meaning.
